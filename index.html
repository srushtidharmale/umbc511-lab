🌱Infancy and Early Childhood Development Lab

Department of Psychology, University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC)

At UMBC’s Infancy and Early Childhood Development Lab, we explore how babies and young children grow, think, and communicate in the earliest years of life. Our research focuses on language development, cognitive milestones, emotional understanding, and social learning in children ages 0–5..

We study how infants learn words, how memory and attention develop, how growing up in bilingual environments affects brain development, and how emotional cues from caregivers shape early communication.

🧪 Want to be part of our research? We invite families with young children to participate in fun, engaging studies that help advance developmental science..
